\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Makes subtitle appear that corresponds to an audio clip from a struct and disappear once the clip has ended. 

Play\+Audio(id) plays a subtitle according to an enum that links to an audio clip and the text for the subtitle. Subtitle\+Reset(time\+To\+Wait) makes the subtitle text blank after time\+To\+Wait seconds.
